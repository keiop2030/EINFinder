About EIN Finder

Purpose

EIN Finder is a web application designed to help businesses and individuals find Employer Identification Numbers (EINs) for businesses they need to file 1099 tax forms for. The tool searches publicly available databases to locate EIN information.

Data Sources

This application searches the following public databases:

  • IRS Tax Exempt Organization Search (TEOS): Database of non-profit organizations with publicly disclosed EINs
  • SEC EDGAR: Securities and Exchange Commission database containing EINs for publicly traded companies

Use Cases

  • Finding EINs for vendors and contractors when preparing 1099 forms
  • Verifying business tax identification numbers
  • Research on publicly available business information
